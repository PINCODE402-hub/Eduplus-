Common Questions

Admissions FAQ

Is there an entrance exam?
Only from Basic 1 upward, and it's a short, friendly placement check rather than a pass/fail exam — it simply helps us place your child in the right class group. Creche and Nursery have no assessment at all.
Can my child join mid-term?
Yes — we accept new students throughout the year, subject to space in the relevant class. Mid-term joiners get a short settling-in period with extra check-ins from the homeroom teacher.
Do you offer sibling discounts?
Yes, families enrolling more than one child receive a discount on the second and subsequent children's fees. Ask our admissions office for current rates.
What are the school hours?
Primary and JHS run 7:00am to 2:30pm, Monday to Friday. Creche and Nursery run a shorter day, 7:30am to 1:00pm, with optional extended care available until 4:00pm.
Is transportation provided?
We operate school bus routes covering most of Sekondi-Takoradi. Ask our office for the current route map and whether your area is covered.
