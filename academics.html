One Campus, One Journey

From first steps to junior high

A child can begin at Daniel Memorial in Creche and graduate JHS 3 from the very same campus — the same community, watching them grow the whole way.

1

Ages 6 months – 2 yrs

Creche

A warm, secure first environment away from home, focused on care, comfort, and early sensory play.

  • Nap & feeding routines matched to each child
  • Sensory play & early motor skills
2

Ages 3 – 5

Nursery 1 & 2

Where curiosity gets its first structure — letters, numbers, songs, and the beginnings of independence.

  • Phonics & early numeracy
  • Music, movement & social play
3

Basic 1 – 6

Primary School

A full academic foundation in literacy, numeracy, science and creative arts, alongside daily character formation.

  • English, Maths, Science, ICT & French
  • Creative arts & physical education
4

Basic 7 – 9

Junior High School

Rigorous preparation for the BECE, with mentorship toward each student's next school and next chapter.

  • Full BECE subject curriculum
  • Career & senior high mentorship
