What's Included

Each uniform set includes

Checked Shirt / Blouse

Sea-blue check for Primary; solid navy with school crest for JHS.

Bottom Wear

Khaki shorts (boys) or pleated skirt (girls), cream-gold trim.

House Tie

JHS students wear a house-colored tie; Primary students do not.

Footwear

Black school shoes and plain white socks, worn daily.
