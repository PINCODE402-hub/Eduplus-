Our Story

A school built on one family's promise

Daniel Memorial Academy was founded in memory of Mr. Daniel Kweku Mensah, a schoolteacher who believed every child deserved a classroom where discipline and warmth could coexist. What began as a single Creche classroom has grown into a full Creche-to-JHS campus, still guided by the same conviction.

01

Whole-child formation

Academics, values, and pastoral care are treated as one curriculum, not three.

02

Small, known classrooms

Every child is known by name — by their teacher, and by the head of school.

03

A steady hand into JHS

The same school, the same community, from a child's first day to their BECE.
